Welcome to

Web Coffee

Friendly neighbourhood cafe

The café was opened in July 2020 (unfortunate timing!). After many years in hospitality industry they decided to make their vision a reality in Web Coffee. Awarded Best Café in Munster, Best Café in Limerick and Best Emerging Cuisine in Limerick 2022 in the Irish Restaurant Association Awards just after two years of running, shows the passion, dedication and hard work of the whole team and the aim to do things simply but right. The café seats 24 inside, and 20 outside (Irish weather permitting). Open all year round (except of an occasional week off season).
